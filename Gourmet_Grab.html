Gourmet Grab

Gourmet Grab is a fictional subscription company as their purpose is for their customers to have the best experience when it comes to preparing and tasting various meals they desire. Every month, a customer will get a box filled with fresh produce that are used to create the meal they choose to make along with instructions to follow along. The meals consists of foods that varies from American, to Japanese.

As someone who enjoys cooking, but don't know what to actually cook or wants to try out a meal, having a meal that is already prepped or comes with inegrdiants that are pre cut not sounds so convinient to those who doesn't like the hassle of cooking, but also those who are interested in cooking something different. In the end, I came up with a logo design that not only has a company name that is engaging and easy to remember, but also something for customers to get excited for.
