LHES T-Shirt Design

Established in 1938 on the island of Lana'i, Lana'i High Elementary School is a public school that consists of Grades K-12, where students of Lana'i residents attend to get the education needed.

Being born and raised on Lana'i for almost all of my life, I attended Lana'i High Elementary School since Kindergarden as I remembered the moments I had with those within my class up until we were in high school as we basically known each other for majority of our childhood.

When I was in contact with one of my former advisors from my school regarding creating a t-shirt design, as an alumini, I immediately took the opportunity as I got started as soon as possible. From how I see it, doing this project was my saying of thank you to those within my school for their help and support while I was going through my struggles, as well as showing my skills that would inspire the current students in the school. This design, compared to the other designs done over the years, presents the primary colors of the school, green and yellow, with a modern twist.
