Maui Vanilla Company

The Maui Vanilla Company is a small business located in the island of Maui as they are known for growing Hawaiian Red Vanilla (Red Split). Once harvested, they are created as organic vanilla extract, great to make baked goods, dessserts, and various dishes.

After months of collaborating with the founder of the company for a final project in college, I got an email back from them to continue to work with the design I created for them. Since then, after months of emailing back and forth of revising the design and colors chosen, it narrowed down to this final draft as it continues to be their official logo for their company as of right now.
