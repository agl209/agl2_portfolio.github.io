Rolling Wellness



Rolling Wellness is a small business that its purpose is to train people into becoming their best selves. Years after coming back to Lana'i, Mr. and Mrs. Rylan and Gabriella Echalar, with years of experience of learning and teaching various Martial Arts back in Arizona, they have continued their training on Lana'i as they would frequently have people over to schedule a day to workout. That is when they decided to create it as a small business to gain awareness within the Lana'i community and reach as much as possible (especially adults and seniors) to consistently train. By reaching many people within the community, they will be able to have maintain a consistent exercise routine to stay active and become their better selves.

My experience working with these people has taught me not only what they specialized in and the importance of being active in the long run, especially when you hit your mid adult years. Having to listen to their story as well as their ideas on what to include in their design, gave me a list of ideas that eventually were narrowed down and resulted in what has been presented below.
