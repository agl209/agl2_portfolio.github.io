Arabela's Portfolio - Project Details

Background

When creating your own portfolio, you the creator have the ability to put out your best work. This would apply to applying for a business or organization during or after graduating school, or for your own business to share with furture clients.

And as someone who have some experiences with creating portfolios and a website dedicated to my best work I have done over the years, I have the gist of knowing and understanding what work I need to include in my portfolio that aligns with the job position or just presenting my best works in general.

However, when it comes to creating a portfolio website that revolves around creating the wireframes, mockup and the actual website through coding, it still continues to be a learning experience for me.



Problem

The problem revolves around the Hows and Whys of getting your portfolio to stick out, which these days, it can be competitive when it comes to getting your portfolio picked out of millions of other portfolios that are sent to companies or organizations you might have applied. And with getting a job that requires a resume and portfolio, getting recognized or hired by a plethora of applicants are slim.


Solution









The solution for this problem would involve creating a protfolio that would be distinctive and distinguish. This is for target audiences to clearly differentate which artist's website is which, whether through use of color schemes, eye-catching thumbnails, or adding and highlighting important words when putting in text paragraphs.
